## What This Post Is About
Day trips and underrated experiences OUTSIDE of San Francisco proper. Lina argues that what makes an SF trip truly unforgettable is the world within a short drive of the city — from ocean cliffs to redwood forests.

---

## Specific Locations Mentioned

### 📍 Cypress Tree Tunnel — Point Reyes National Seashore
- That iconic arching tree tunnel, famous for golden-light photography
- "Even better in person when the light comes through"
- ~1 hr north of SF

### 📍 Pigeon Point Lighthouse — Near Half Moon Bay
- One of the tallest lighthouses in the US
- Sits directly on the coast with nonstop ocean views
- ~1 hr south of SF — directly on our route

### 📍 Fjord Spa — Sausalito
- Scandinavian-style spa right by the water
- Pitched as perfect for a slower, more relaxing day
- ~30 min north of SF (same direction as Muir Woods)

### 📍 Napa Valley Wine Train
- Elevated, vintage-feeling dining experience
- "Feels like stepping into a different era"
- ~1.5 hrs north of SF — possible detour only

### 📍 Hakone Estate and Gardens — Saratoga
- Peaceful Japanese garden, very removed from California tourist energy
- ~1 hr south of SF (toward Santa Cruz)

### 📍 Pulgas Water Temple — Redwood City
- Underrated architectural stop
- ~40 min south of SF

### 📍 Roaring Camp Railroads — Felton (near Santa Cruz)
- **KEY FIND**: Narrow-gauge steam train ride through old-growth redwood forest
- "Feels straight out of a storybook"
- ~1.5 hrs south of SF — directly en route toward Big Sur
- **Relevance**: Strong alternative redwood experience much closer than Trees of Mystery

### 📍 Capitola Village — Santa Cruz County
- Colorful pastel seaside village that "looks like it belongs somewhere in Europe"
- ~1.5 hrs south of SF, near Santa Cruz
- Great for families, very photogenic, walkable

---

## Trip Relevance
- **Pigeon Point Lighthouse** → On our route, pairs with Half Moon Bay Day
- **Cypress Tree Tunnel** → Potential Day 1 or 2 morning activity heading north toward Marin/Muir Woods
- **Roaring Camp Railroads** → Strong redwood substitute or addition on the way south from SF, before Big Sur
- **Capitola Village** → Potential stop between SF and Carmel/Monterey
