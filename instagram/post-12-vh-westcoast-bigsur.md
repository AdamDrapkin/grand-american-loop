## Key Specific Big Sur Stops (NOW CONFIRMED BY MULTIPLE SOURCES)

### 📍 Bixby Creek Bridge
- Confirmed across Posts 12, 14, and 15 — this is the non-negotiable Big Sur stop
- Best viewed from the north pullout (turnout just north of the bridge on Hwy 1)
- ~26 miles south of Carmel-by-the-Sea

### 📍 McWay Falls — Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park
- An 80-foot waterfall that drops directly onto a beach cove
- One of the only waterfalls in California that falls onto a beach
- Accessible via short (~0.5 mile) Overlook Trail
- **Cannot access the beach** — view from above only
- Entry fee: ~$12/vehicle for Julia Pfeiffer Burns State Park
- Located ~37 miles south of Carmel, before arriving at Big Sur village

Bixby Bridge and McWay Falls are now confirmed as the two specific must-do stops on the Big Sur driving day. Both are dramatic, family-accessible (short walk to McWay), and deeply photogenic. These are now locked into the Big Sur day planning.
