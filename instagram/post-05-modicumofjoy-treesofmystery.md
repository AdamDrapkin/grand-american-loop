## HOT TIPS (From Creator)
- **Arrive at 9AM** — almost no crowds, hassle-free experience
- **Check their webcam** — faces the parking lot, shows crowd levels AND weather before you drive up
- **Free parking** next to the iconic Paul Bunyan & Babe the Blue Ox statues
- By noon, the lot fills to capacity — 9AM arrival is not optional
- **Wear sturdy shoes** — bridges sway, decks are uneven
- **Dress in layers** — canopy temperatures are cooler than ground level

## The Experience
- Traverse 8 suspension bridges 50–100 feet above the forest floor
- Complete gondola ride above the full redwood canopy
- Old-growth redwoods — the same ancient trees the family wants to see
- Named in hashtags: #redwoodsnationalpark — this IS the Redwood National Park area

## Route Planning Consideration
If this is what the family is envisioning by "Redwood," we have two options:
1. **Start the trip here** (fly into SF, drive 6 hrs north on Day 1, spend Day 1–2 at Trees of Mystery and Redwood NP parks, then drive back south through SF) — adds 12+ hours of driving to the trip
2. **Substitute with closer redwood experiences**: Muir Woods (45 min from SF), Roaring Camp Railroads in Felton (1.5 hrs from SF), or Big Basin Redwoods (1.5 hrs from SF) — all old-growth redwoods, much less driving
