## Key NEW Destinations Surfaced

### 📍 Hearst Castle — San Simeon, CA
- Grand historic estate built by William Randolph Hearst, sitting on the hills above the Pacific
- Located between Big Sur and Paso Robles — **directly on our route**
- Now mentioned in both Post 11 and Post 15 — consistent signal from multiple high-engagement creators
- Requires timed tour reservations
- **Trip relevance: Strong candidate for Day 8 (Big Sur → Paso Robles) as a midday stop**

### 📍 Kings Canyon National Park
- Adjacent to and managed with Sequoia National Park
- Can be visited as part of the same Sequoia day
- Adds depth to the giant tree experience without requiring extra driving
