## Key NEW Specific Stops

### 📍 Moro Rock — Sequoia National Park
- A massive granite dome you can climb via a staircase trail (~0.5 miles round trip, ~350 steps)
- 360° panoramic views of the Sierra Nevada and San Joaquin Valley
- Family-friendly — the steps are paved/carved into the rock
- Located in the Giant Forest area, near General Sherman Tree
- **Can combine with General Sherman on the same Sequoia day**

### 📍 Lombard Street — San Francisco
- The famous "world's crookiest street" with 8 hairpin turns
- Short drive/walk, very photogenic, kids enjoy it
- Located in Russian Hill, 10 minutes from Fisherman's Wharf

### 📍 El Capitan — Yosemite Valley
- Viewable for free from the valley floor
- One of the world's most famous rock climbing destinations
- The sheer scale is what makes it memorable — no hiking required to see it
