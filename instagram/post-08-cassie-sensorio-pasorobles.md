## Location

- Set on 35 acres of hillside
- Thousands of glowing orb lights spread across the hillside

## The Experience
- Walk through thousands of illuminated fiber-optic orbs spread across 35 rolling acres
- Designed by Bruce Munro (world-renowned light artist)
- Best visited at **sunset** — watch the field transform from daylight to full illumination
- "Way more impressive in person than photos even show"
- Fully family-friendly, outdoor, walking-pace
