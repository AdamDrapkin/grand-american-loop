Afternoon — Chinatown & North Waterfront
  • Chinatown (Grant Avenue + Stockton Street) — 45 min to 1 hour. America's oldest Chinatown. Dragon Gate at Bush & Grant Ave is the landmark entry. Gold-coin trinkets, incense shops, dim sum bakeries. Stockton St is the working street (produce, live fish) — more authentic than touristy Grant Ave.
Palace of Fine Arts
3601 Lyon St, San Francisco Free 30–45 min

The colonnade and dome over the lagoon — one of the most photogenic structures in SF. Originally built for the 1915 Panama-Pacific Exposition. The reflection in the lagoon in afternoon light is exceptional. Walk the colonnade perimeter, photograph across the water.

📍 Palace of Fine Arts
Powell-Hyde Cable Car
Powell & Market turnaround · Fisherman's Wharf $24/adult all-day Muni passport Unlimited transit all day

Board at Powell & Market Street (the turnaround where they spin the cars manually — worth watching). Ride up and over the hill to Fisherman's Wharf. The Powell-Hyde line goes over the steepest grades — better views than Powell-Mason. The $24 all-day Muni passport covers all buses, streetcars, and cable cars for the rest of the day.

The turnaround at Powell & Market has a line in the morning. Take the turnaround at Fisherman's Wharf (Hyde & Beach) instead — board there to go back into the city with no wait.
📍 Powell & Market Turnaround
Late Afternoon — Russian Hill & Alamo Square
  • Lombard Street (Russian Hill) — the famous crooked block between Hyde and Leavenworth. 20 min stop. Walk down (not drive) for the best photos of the switchbacks. Or walk up from the bottom and look back at the city framing.
  • Painted Ladies (710–720 Steiner St, Alamo Square Park) — the six Victorian houses with downtown SF skyline behind them. Best from the park bench row on Steiner facing east. 20 min.
Sunset — Twin Peaks
Twin Peaks Sunset Viewpoint
Twin Peaks Blvd, San Francisco Free 20 min

Panoramic 360° view of the Bay from a short drive south of the Mission. The city grid, the Bay Bridge, and (on clear days) the Golden Gate. Sunset here on arrival day sets the stage for everything that follows. Short walk from the parking lot to the summit.

Alcatraz Island — Audio Tour
Pier 33 Alcatraz Landing · 9:20 AM departure ✅ Booked · Conf. i77487162 Return ~12:00 PM · ~2.5 hrs on island

The cell house audio tour narrated by former guards and prisoners is the core experience — the actual voices describing life inside. Do the full route: cell house, recreation yard, library, D-block (solitary). Plan 2–2.5 hours. The views of the Bay and SF skyline from the island are extraordinary.

Arrive at Pier 33 by 8:35 AM — 45 min before departure. Boarding closes before departure time. QR code valid for the entire party (on one mobile device). No weapons, no oversized bags. Non-refundable.

Bring: Headphones (app-based audio tour), fully charged phone, backpack max 16"×20", closed-toe shoes (the island is uneven). The audio tour app is free — download before leaving the hotel.

Fort Point National Historic Site
201 Marine Dr, San Francisco, CA 94129 Free Admission Fri 10:00 AM–5:00 PM 45–55 min

Built 1853–1861 directly beneath the south tower of the Golden Gate Bridge — the only pre-Civil War brick fort on the West Coast. Standing inside the fort looking straight up at the Golden Gate span above is one of the most surreal viewpoints in SF. The fort itself is fascinating: arched brick casemates, cannon placements, the narrow stairwells.

@modicumofjoy captured this view (377,503 plays, 23,170 likes). The angle looking up at the bridge from the fort's top level is the shot.

📍 Fort Point NHS
Depart Fort Point by ~1:15 PM sharp to maintain the Berkeley timing. Fort Point closes at 5 PM — you have plenty of time with 45–55 min here.

UC Berkeley Campus
2200 Piedmont Ave, Berkeley, CA Campanile: $5/person Sproul Plaza: Free ~1 hour · Depart 3:00 PM sharp

Sather Tower (Campanile) — $5/person to ride the elevator to the observation deck. 307 feet. Panoramic views of the Bay, SF skyline, and Berkeley hills. Worth the $5. The carillon plays at 7:50 AM, 12:00 PM, and 6:00 PM — if you're here around noon you'll hear it.

Sproul Plaza — the epicenter of Free Speech Movement history (1964). The steps, the plaza. Walk through, see the energy of the campus. Free.

Bancroft Way and Telegraph Ave: cafes, bookstores, Berkeley street life. Good place for a quick lunch/coffee before the Muir Woods drive.

Leave Berkeley at 3:00 PM sharp. The Muir Woods parking slot is 4:00–4:30 PM and the drive is 65 min with typical Friday afternoon traffic. Missing the slot is a real risk.

Muir Woods National Monument
✅ Booked · 4:00–4:30 PM parking slot $15/person · gomuirwoods.com NPS hours: 8 AM–8 PM (May–Sep) ~2 hours

Old-growth coast redwoods — the closest ancient redwood grove to SF. Cathedral Grove Trail is 1 mile flat on a boardwalk through trees 250–1,000 years old. The canopy closes overhead and the light shifts. This is the warm-up for the giants you'll see more of on the Big Sur drive.

Parking is required in advance (your slot is confirmed). No cell service inside the park — download the offline NPS map before leaving Berkeley. Auto-theft is common in the parking lot — remove all valuables from the car.

Arrive at your parking slot at 4:00–4:15 PM. You have until closing (8 PM) — roughly 2 hours comfortably. The Cathedral Grove and Bohemian Grove loops are the essential trails. No need to rush.

Sterling Vineyards — The Sterling Stroll
✅ Booked · Conf. TOCK-R-AE87DB44 10:45 AM · Arrive by 10:30 AM 1111 Dunaweal Ln, Calistoga, CA 94515 $192.53 paid · Visa 9942 ~1 hour · Depart ~11:45 AM

Sterling sits on a hilltop reached by aerial gondola tram — the gondola ride itself is the signature (@discover_california_, 246K, 109,398 views). The Sterling Stroll is a self-guided walking experience through the winery: six wine stations, stunning Napa Valley panoramas, architecture designed around the hillside site.

Booking: Sterling Stroll ×2 + Non-taster ×1 + Guest 10–20 ×1. The reservation is for 10:45 AM — arrive at the gondola base by 10:30 AM.

Rules: No outside food or alcohol, no pets (service animals OK), no strong perfume/cologne near the wine. Gondola pauses if temperature exceeds 100°F.

Manage or reschedule at tock.com (search TOCK-R-AE87DB44). Can reschedule or cancel up to 1 day before. Can transfer to another person.
